Local Hack Day 2016 - Temple University

This project was completed by a team of graduate students from Temple University as a part of Local Hack Day 2016.

Project Prompt

The purpose of the project was to provide an effective scheduling algorithm for a medical transportation service to pick up and drop off patients at appointments.

Challenges and Accomplishments

The team struggled with connecting to the database, and settling on an effective algorithm to represent the scheduling logic. Ultimately, a solid design for the algorithm was developed, but time was too limited to realize a full implementation in the web app.
